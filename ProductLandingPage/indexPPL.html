All about spices

There are several different spices used to make the traditional desi tea.

Cardamom: The beauty of this spice is that it adds a sweet and citrusy flavour to the tea. Even though the flavour is very strong, its perfect for your traditional desi Chai.

Star Anise: As mentioned in the name of the spice, this spice is a star shaped. Even though this spice is mainly used in Chinese cooking, using this in your tea adds a licorice flavour.

Cinnamon: Now who doesnt love a bit of cinnamon. Adding a cinnoman stick to the tea brings a soothingly sweet tone to the tea with its slight citrusy notes and spicy taste it adds a full-bodied flavour if used along side the other spices.

Peppercorn: The spicy kick of black peppercorns is amazing to used in your chai if you love that little spicy kick in your tea.

Cloves: The musty flavour of cloves is a reason why it is mainly used in chai. Hence why it is a staple when your making your desi tea.

Nutmeg: Being a warm spice, nutmeg has a nutty and sweet flavour. It is quite an aromatic spice which gives your tea the desi chai smell. A small amount of this will do wonders to your tea.

Quick recipe for the perfect desi chai

  1. In a pot, boil 1 cup of water
  2. Add in your teabag alongside our Chai Time spices
  3. Once the water comes to a boil, add your milk
  4. Let this simmer on a low heat for 2-3minutes
  5. Pour the tea into a cup using a sieve and ENJOY
