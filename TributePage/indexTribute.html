Spider Man

An inspiration to all

Spiderman is a character that was created by Marvel comics in 1962. Peter parker (a young boy) was bitten by a radioactive spider from which he gained superhuman strength, speed and aligity. Alongside this he gained the ability to shoot webs from his fingers. This gave him the name, Spiderman.

Peter Parker (Spider-Man) was played by 3 different actors.

Toby Maguire for Spider-Man, Spider-Man 2 and Spider-Man 3.

Andrew Garfield for The Amazing Spider-Man and The Amazing Spider-Man 2.

Tom Holland for Spider-Man Homecoming, Spider-Man: Far From Home and Spider-Man: No Way Home.

Spider-Man also appeared in the Captain America: Civil War, Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers:Endgame.

    Here's a timeline of the Spider-Man movies:
  • 2002 Spider-Man - First ever Spider-Man movie was released. Green Goblin was the first villan to appear in the Spider-Man movies.
  • 2004 Spider-Man 2 - Second installment of Spider-Man. This movie was set 2 years after the original Spider-Man movie and focused on the struggles Peter Parker faced trying to manage his personal life and his duties as Spider-Man. The villan in this movie was Dr. Otto Octavius.
  • 2007 Spider-Man 3 - The final Spider-Man movie for this movies sequel. In this movie he fights against 3 villans. Venom, Sandman and New goblin. New goblin is Peter Parker's friend Harry Osborn who seeks vegenance against Spider-Man for his fathers death.
  • 2012 The Amazing Spider-Man - A new introduction to Spider-Man. Same as the first ever Spider-Man movie released, Peter-Parker gets bitten by a spider which gives him superpowers. Spider-Man fights Dr. Curt Connors alter ego "The Lizard" who is the villan in this movie.
  • 2014 The Amazing Spider-Man 2 - In this movie, Peter comes face to face with a new villan Electro and an old friend Harry Osborn.
  • 2016 Captain America: Civil War - Spider-Man is introduced into the MCU. Peter gets recruited by Tony Stark (Iron Man) for the upcoming battle.
  • 2017 Spider-Man: Homecoming - After making his debut in Captain America: Civil War, Peter starts to navigate his new-found identity. Vulture - the new villan in this movie is introduced who threatens everything Spider-Man holds important.
  • 2018 Avengers: Infinity War - Spider-Man alongisde the other Avengers go to war against Thanos.
  • 2019 Avengers: Endgame - Spider-Man makes a reappearance in Endgame alongside the other Avengers.
  • 2019 Spider-Man: Far From Home - Peter goes on a Europe vacation alongside his best friends MJ and Ned aswell as his classmates. He agrees to help Nick Fury uncover the mystery of several elemental attacks which inturn creates havoc. He goes up against Mysterio (who he assumed was one of the good guys).
  • 2021 Spider-Man: No Way Home - With his identity revealed to the world for the first time in cinematic history, Peter is faced with conflict with his normal life and those who he cares about that are now at risk. With the help of Dr Strange to restore his secret, Spider-Man is faced with all the villans Spider-Man has fought within the multiverse.
